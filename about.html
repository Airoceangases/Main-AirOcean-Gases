Our Mission? A Clear Goal:

To provide exceptional gas solutions, become the most trusted independent gas distributor, leveraging our expertise, high-quality products, and personalized service to drive the success of our customers across industries.

Founding of Hongyuan Gas

Hongyuan Gas Technology Co., Ltd. is established in Luoyang, Henan Province, China, with a mission to supply high-quality industrial gases to local manufacturing and medical sectors.

Expanding Capabilities

Hongyuan invests in advanced cryogenic separation units, increasing production capacity for oxygen, nitrogen, and argon to meet growing national demand.

Entering Specialty Gases

Introduction of high-purity helium, neon, krypton, and xenon production lines, supplying China’s electronics, semiconductor, and research industries.

Technology & Certification Milestones

Facilities upgraded with automated filling lines, GMP certification obtained, and full ISO Quality Management system implemented, ensuring global-level quality and safety.

Airocean Gases Launch

Airocean Gases, LLC is established in the United States to serve the North American market, bringing Hongyuan’s 40+ years of expertise overseas.

Health and Safety

We are committed to providing a safe and healthy working environment for employees, customers, and visitors. As the largest independent gas distributor on the West Coast, we have established a comprehensive health and safety management system to ensure that all business activities meet the highest safety standards.

We emphasize the concept of sustainable development and have established a positive safety culture that is supported by the company’s owners, management, and every employee. Through continuous safety training and strict execution standards, We ensure that every stage meets the highest safety standards.

Leadership

Board Member

Wentao Guo

Wentao Guo is one of the founding Board Members of Airocean Gases, LLC, the U.S.-based overseas subsidiary of Yichuan Hongyuan Gas Technology Co., Ltd. He came to the United States in 2016 to study Accounting at Iowa State University and later earned a Master’s degree in Business Analytics from Northeastern University in Boston.

With professional experience in accounting roles at two U.S. companies, Wentao brings strong expertise in finance, compliance, and operations. In 2024, he co-founded Airocean Gases, LLC to lead the family business’s global expansion, leveraging his academic background and cross-cultural insight to drive sustainable growth in overseas markets.

Board Member

Wenhao Guo

Wenhao Guo is one of the founding Board Members of Airocean Gases, LLC, dedicated to delivering high-quality industrial gases and innovative solutions to customers worldwide. He is also the second-generation leader of his family-owned industrial gas business.

In 2022 and 2023, Wenhao earned his Bachelor’s degree in Management Information Systems @ University of Delaware and Master’s degree in Business Analytics @ University of Maryland - College Park, respectively. In 2022, Wenhao began preparing the launch plan for Airocean Gases with his team, while also working as a Process & Forecast Specialist at a manufacturing company in the supply chain sector.

Building on this educational and professional background, he brings extensive expertise, global supply chain resources, and a customer-first philosophy to the U.S. market.

Headquartered in the United States, Airocean Gases LLC specializes in providing safe, reliable, and efficient gas products and services for the industrial, medical, semiconductor, and food industries — serving local needs with a global vision.

Board Member

Jiahao Guo

Jiahao Guo is one of the founding Board Members of Airocean Gases, LLC, carrying forward his family’s legacy in the industrial and specialty gases sector, where the family has been deeply rooted for decades. Building on this heritage, Jiahao is committed to connecting global gas supply with the U.S. market through reliable sourcing, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service.

Jiahao holds a Bachelor’s degree in Finance from Arizona State University, a Master’s degree in Financial Analysis from King’s College London, and a Master’s degree in Management and Systems from New York University. Combining his strong academic background with hands-on industry experience, he brings expertise in data analysis, business management, and international market development.

With Air Ocean Gases, Jiahao’s mission is to deliver high-purity rare gases, industrial gases, and related equipment to industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, medical applications, and research institutions. He is dedicated to building a trustworthy supply chain, enhancing operational efficiency, and fostering long-term partnerships with clients worldwide.
