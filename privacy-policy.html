3. The Data We Collect About You

Personal data or personal information is any information that can identify a person. It does not include data that has been deleted (anonymous data).

We may collect, use, store, and transmit different types of personal data about you, which we categorize as follows:

  • Identity Data includes your name, username, or similar identifiers.
  • Contact Data includes your billing address, delivery address, email address, and phone number.
  • Technical Data includes your internet protocol (IP) address, login data, browser type and version, time zone setting, location, browser plug-in type and version, operating system and platform, and other technology on the device you use to access our website.
  • Usage Data includes information about how you use our website, products, and services.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They allow websites to recognize your device and remember whether you have visited the website before.

Our Use of Cookies

We use cookies from our customer relationship management (CRM) platform to help us understand how visitors use our website. These cookies collect information such as:

  • How you reached our website
  • Which pages you visit
  • How long you stay on each page
  • What you click on in our website
  • When you return to our website

These cookies may be stored on your device for up to two years, but some may be deleted sooner.

Your Cookie Choices

You can set your browser to reject all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being set on a website. If you disable or reject cookies, please note that some parts of this website may not be accessible or function properly.
